About the Movie

Rhythm of the Islands tells the inspiring story of Jared Lewis, a young musician from Port of Spain, Trinidad. Raised by his hardworking mother, Sheila, and inspired by the legacy of his late father, a steelpan player, Jared dreams of winning the prestigious Road March competition.

Jared’s journey takes a turn when he meets Alyssa Phillips, a gifted dancer from a wealthy background. Though initially at odds, their shared love for music and performance unites them. Just as Jared begins to rise, tragedy strikes with Sheila's passing, leaving him devastated.

With support from Alyssa , Jared channels his grief into his music, creating songs that touch hearts across his community. The story culminates in a dazzling Road March performance, blending Soca and steelpan music to honor his parents’ legacy. Crowned Road March King, Jared celebrates not only his triumph but the power of resilience and cultural heritage. The film closes with a jubilant Carnival parade, showcasing the vibrant spirit of Trinidad and Tobago.
